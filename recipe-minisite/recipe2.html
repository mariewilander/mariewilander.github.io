Esquites

An off-the-cob version of elotes, grilled on-the-cob Mexican street corn with a creamy, cheesy, lime and chile flavored sauce.

Cook Time: 20 mins

Total Time: 20 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 ears fresh corn, shucked, kernals removed (about 3 cups)
  • Salt
  • 2 ounces feta or Cotija cheese, finely crumbled
  • ½ cup finely sliced scallions, green parts only
  • ½ cup fresh cilantro leaves, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and stemmed, finely chopped
  • 1 to 2 medium cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • Chile powder or hot chile flakes, to taste

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet with high heat until shimmering. Add corn kernels and season to taste with salt. Cook without moving until charred and toss. Cook until corn is well charred, about 10 minutes. Transfer to a large bowl.
  2. To your large bowl of corn, add feta or Cotija cheese, scallions, cilantro, jalapeno, garlic, mayonnaise, lime juice, and chile powder. Toss to combine. Taste and adjust seasoning with more salt and more chile powder to taste. Serve immediately.

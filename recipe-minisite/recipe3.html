Buffalo Chex Mix

A savory and salty snack with a buttery ranch seasoning!

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 30 mins

Total Time: 40 mins

Servings: approximately 16

Ingredients

  • 5 cups Rice Chex
  • 5 cups Corn Chex/li>
  • ⅓-½ cup Frank's Red Hot Buffalo Sauce
  • 2 cups Ranch Groove Cheez-Its
  • 1 cup Gardetto's rye chips or regular Gardetto's
  • 2 cups buffalo Snyder pretzel pieces
  • 1 packet Hidden Valley ranch seasoning
  • ¼ cup melted butter

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 300F
  2. In a bowl, mix the melted butter, buffalo sauce, and ranch seasoning. In a large bowl, pour chex mix and add butter mixture; fold until evenly coated.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients listed and fold in. Spread evenly on a greased baking sheet and bake for 15 minutes.
  4. Remove from oven and break up the pieces. Bake again for another 15 minutes. If chex mix is still mostly soggy, bake for another 5-10 minutes but keep an eye on it.
  5. Let cool.
